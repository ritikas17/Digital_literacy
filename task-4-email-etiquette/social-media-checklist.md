
#5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student.

#Do’s

Use respectful and professional language

Protect your privacy (adjust settings)

Think before posting content

Share useful and positive information

Verify facts before sharing

#Don’ts

Don’t share personal or sensitive information

Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content

Don’t spread fake news or rumors

Don’t engage in online arguments or bullying

Don’t use weak or shared passwords
