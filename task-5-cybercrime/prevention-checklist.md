# Stay Safe Online Checklist (For College Students in India)

## General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Do not share personal information (address, phone number, OTPs) publicly
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files
- Verify information before sharing to prevent misinformation
- Log out from accounts on shared or public devices
- Keep apps, devices, and antivirus software updated
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid accessing sensitive accounts

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Always verify payment requests—entering a PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it

## Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
