# Digital Literacy Project

## 📌 Overview
This repository contains a case study on UPI payment fraud and a checklist for staying safe online. It aims to spread awareness about digital safety and responsible internet use among college students.

---

## 📄 Contents

### 1. Case Study: UPI Payment Fraud
- Explains what UPI fraud is
- Step-by-step description of how the fraud happens
- Identifies common targets
- Highlights consequences and prevention tips

📂 File: `casestudy.md`

---

### 2. Stay Safe Online Checklist
- Provides practical tips for safe internet usage
- Includes general online safety practices
- Covers important UPI/financial safety tips
- Lists official cybercrime reporting channels in India

📂 File: `prevention-checklist.md`

---

## 🎯 Purpose
The purpose of this project is to:
- Increase awareness about cybercrime
- Promote safe online behavior
- Help students protect their personal and financial information

---

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline: 1930  

---

## 📚 Conclusion
By following safe digital practices and staying informed, users can reduce the risk of online fraud and maintain a secure digital presence.
